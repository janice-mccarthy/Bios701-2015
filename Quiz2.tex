\begin{enumerate}
\item What is a random variable?\\\\\\\\\\
\item Let $p$ be the probability that a plane crashes (assume that $p$ is the same for all flights and flights are independent). Let $X$ be the number of crashes in $n$ flights. What is the distribution of $X$? Write its pmf.\\\\\\\\\\\\\
\item Consider a deck of 52 playing cards. Thirteen are hearts (red), thirteen are diamonds (red), thirteen are clubs (black) and thirteen are spades (black). Suppose we remove all spades from the deck. From the remaining cards, we draw 10. Let $X$ be the number of red cards drawn. What is the distribution of $X$? Write its pmf.
\end{enumerate}
